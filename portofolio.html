Caveman Studio

The project Cavman Studio was my 2. semester exam and a group project from KEA - Copenhagen School of Technology & Design. We collaborated to design a website for producer and musician Lucas Delacroix, founder of Caveman Studio.

The process started by doing comprehensive research and having a conversation with Lucas about his his goals and requirements. We then moved into a design sprint, ultimately developing a complete design system and prototype in Figma. The site was built using the Astro framework, with Supabase providing database integration, and used JavaScript to fetch data from the database to make the website dynamic.

Additionally, we produced photos and videos for social media, which are also featured on the website.

GirlTalk

Another team project from KEA - Copenhagen School of Technology & Design, where we made a Webfeature for GirlTalk. GirlTalk is a community that works to prevent well-being issues among girls and young women.

We worked within an existing design system, adhering to predefined color schemes and fonts. Our team followed the design sprint process, developing user stories and sketches. Using the Astro framework, we built the website's frontend. The project also involved creating custom infographics and graphic elements, all designed by our team to enhance the website's visuals.

QR Kode

My QR-Code Generator is a project I made after working in Tech & Storylab, as a Student Assistant. Here I need to make a lot of different QR-Codes, in different colors, and I was tired of searching for working generators on Google, so I decide to make my own.

By the help of YouTube and GitHub, I developed this fully customizable generator in HMTL, CSS and JavaScript.
